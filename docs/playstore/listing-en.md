## Full Description (max 4000 characters)

TouchPC turns your Android phone into a wireless remote control for your PC. Control the mouse, type on the keyboard, and execute keyboard shortcuts right from the palm of your hand -- all through your local WiFi network.

--- HOW IT WORKS ---

1. Download and install the TouchPC server on your PC (available on our website).
2. Connect your phone and PC to the same WiFi network.
3. Open TouchPC on your phone and enter your PC's IP address.
4. Confirm the connection using the security PIN displayed on the server.
5. Start controlling your PC remotely.

--- KEY FEATURES ---

VIRTUAL TOUCHPAD
- Move your PC cursor by sliding your finger across the screen.
- Single tap for left click.
- Double tap for double click.
- Two-finger tap for right click.
- Three-finger tap for middle click.
- Two-finger scroll (vertical and horizontal).
- Two-finger pinch gesture for zoom.
- Three-finger swipe for system gestures.
- Long press and drag for drag-and-drop.

FULL KEYBOARD
- Complete QWERTY keyboard with all standard keys.
- Dedicated function keys tab (F1-F12).
- Integrated numeric keypad (numpad).
- Text input field for typing full sentences and sending them to the PC.
- Modifier keys: Ctrl, Alt, Shift, Win.

QUICK SHORTCUTS
- Copy (Ctrl+C), Paste (Ctrl+V), Cut (Ctrl+X).
- Undo (Ctrl+Z) and Redo (Ctrl+Y).
- Select All (Ctrl+A).
- Save (Ctrl+S), Find (Ctrl+F).
- New Tab (Ctrl+T), Close Tab (Ctrl+W).
- Switch Window (Alt+Tab), Close Program (Alt+F4).
- Show Desktop (Win+D).

CUSTOMIZABLE SETTINGS
- Adjustable cursor sensitivity.
- Configurable movement acceleration.
- Customizable scroll speed.
- Optional natural (inverted) scrolling.
- Tap-to-click toggle.
- Configurable haptic feedback (vibration).
- Light, dark, or system-default theme.

SECURE AND FAST CONNECTION
- Low-latency WebSocket communication.
- PIN-based security authentication.
- Server connection history (up to 10 entries).
- Operates exclusively on local network (LAN) for maximum security.
- No internet required, no user accounts needed.
- Background service for continuous connection.

--- REQUIREMENTS ---

- Android phone running version 8.0 (Oreo) or higher.
- Windows PC running the TouchPC server application.
- Both devices connected to the same local WiFi network.

--- PRIVACY ---

TouchPC does not collect personal data, does not use analytics, contains no trackers, and does not send any information to third parties. All communication happens exclusively within your local network. Your privacy is fully protected.
