Privacy Policy for TouchPC - Remote Control

Effective Date: February 21, 2026 | Last Updated: February 21, 2026
App Name: TouchPC - Remote Control | Package: com.touchpc.remotecontrol

Overview

TouchPC - Remote Control ("the App") is a utility application that allows users to control their PC's keyboard and mouse from an Android device over a local WiFi network.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. The App is designed to operate entirely within your local network and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with external parties.

Data Collection

The App does not collect any personal data.

  • Does NOT collect any personally identifiable information (PII).
  • Does NOT collect usage analytics or statistics.
  • Does NOT use any third-party analytics services.
  • Does NOT contain advertising or ad-tracking SDKs.
  • Does NOT track user behavior, location, or device information.
  • Does NOT access contacts, camera, microphone, files, or any other sensitive device resources.

Data Stored Locally on Device

The App stores a minimal amount of data locally on your device using Android's DataStore Preferences. This data never leaves your device:

  • Touchpad sensitivity settings: Cursor sensitivity, acceleration, and scroll speed values.
  • App preferences: Tap-to-click, natural scrolling, haptic feedback, and theme selection.
  • Server connection history: IP addresses and port numbers of previously connected PC servers (up to 10 entries).

This data can be cleared at any time through the App's settings or by clearing the App's data through Android system settings.

Network Communication

The App communicates exclusively over your local area network (LAN/WiFi).

  • All data transmission is local. No data is sent to the internet or to any third party.
  • Connection security. PIN-based authentication handshake prevents unauthorized connections.
  • No internet required. Only requires both devices on the same local WiFi network.
  • Data transmitted: Only input control commands (mouse movements, clicks, key presses, scroll events, keyboard shortcuts). No personal data is transmitted.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services. No analytics, advertising, crash reporting, social media, or cloud services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from any users, including children. It is rated for all ages (Everyone) and is safe for use by users of any age group.

Permissions

  • INTERNET / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: For local WebSocket connection to the PC server.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: To maintain persistent connection while in background.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To display active connection notification (Android 13+).
  • VIBRATE: For haptic feedback on touchpad and keyboard interaction.

Data Security

No data traverses the public internet. PIN-based authentication prevents unauthorized connections. Locally stored preferences use Android's private internal storage, accessible only to the App.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above.

Your Rights

Since the App does not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete from our end. All data resides locally on your device under your full control.
